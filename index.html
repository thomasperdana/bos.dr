Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from regular email marketing?
Traditional email marketing has low open rates and even lower response rates. Our multi-channel approach uses SMS, email, and voicemail in a coordinated sequence that dramatically increases engagement. Plus, our AI system personalizes each message based on the lead's previous interactions.
What kind of businesses does this work for?
The 72-Hour Revenue Rally works for any business with a database of previous customers or leads who haven't purchased recently. We've seen success in industries ranging from home services and professional services to e-commerce and B2B companies.
How quickly can I expect to see results?
As the name suggests, you'll start seeing appointment requests within 72 hours of launch. Most clients see their first appointments booked within the first week, with significant revenue generated within the first month.
What happens after the 72-hour campaign?
After your initial successful campaign, we recommend moving to our $297/month SaaS subscription to keep the AI Receptionist running. This ensures continuous nurturing of your database and ongoing appointment generation without additional effort from your team.
How do you ensure the quality of appointments?
Our AI system qualifies leads through a series of questions before scheduling appointments. Only leads who meet your specific criteria and express genuine interest are booked into your calendar, ensuring your time is spent with high-quality prospects.
